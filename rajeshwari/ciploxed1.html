CIPLOX 0.3% EYE DROPS

DESCRIPTION:

Ciplox 0.3 % eye drops is an antibiotic that is used to treat eye infections such as conjunctivitis and corneal ulcers caused by certain types of bacteria.

 Note:
This medicine is not recommended for used in patients less than one year of age.

USES:
  1. --Bacterial conjunctivitis:
    This medicine is used in treatment of conjunctivitis, which is an inflammation or infection of conjunctiva, caused by bacteria such as staphylococcus aureus, Streptococcus, pneumoniae, haemophilia influence, etc..
  2. --Corneal ulcers:
    This medicine is used in the treatment of corneal ulcer, which is an infection of inflammatory condition of cornea, caused by bacteria such as staphylococcus aureus, pneumonia etc..

SIDE EFFECTS:
  1. Blurred vision
  2. Eye Pain
  3. Redness of the eye
  4. Eye Discomfort
  5. Burning or itching of eyes
  6. Dry eye
  7. Swelling of the eye lid

Dosage:

10ml eye ear drops
